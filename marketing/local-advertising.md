- **Claim and fully optimize your Google Business Profile** ‚Äî this is the single highest-ROI free marketing action available

## ü§ù LOCAL BUSINESS PARTNERSHIPS

Cross-promotion with complementary businesses in Standish and Gorham area:

### Tire Shops / Parts Stores
- **AutoZone / O'Reilly / NAPA in Gorham or Portland area** ‚Äî ask if you can leave business cards; some have referral relationships
- Connect with tow companies in the area ‚Äî tow drivers often recommend repair shops

### Car Washes
- Partner with a local car wash for cross-promotions: "Get your car detailed, we'll check your fluids" type of deals

### Real Estate Agents
- New homeowners moving to Standish/Gorham area need a local mechanic immediately
- Connect with RE/MAX, Coldwell Banker agents in the area
- Offer a "Welcome to Standish" packet ‚Äî $10 off first service coupon to new residents

### Insurance Agents
- State Farm, GEICO agents in the Gorham/Standish area
- After an accident, insurance agents often refer clients to trusted repair shops
- Introduce yourself, leave cards

### Rental Companies
- Connecting with property management or vacation rental owners on Sebago Lake ‚Äî seasonal visitors whose cars break down need a local shop fast

### Local Employers
- **Idexx Laboratories, Westbrook** ‚Äî large local employer 20 minutes away
- **MaineHealth / Mercy Hospital, Portland**
- Offer a fleet discount or employee discount program ‚Äî bulk business relationships

### AAA
- Become an AAA Approved Auto Repair shop
- AAA members actively seek out approved shops and trust the designation
- Annual fee, but drives consistent referral traffic
